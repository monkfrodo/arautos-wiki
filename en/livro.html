The Commissariat of the Heralds of the Gospel -- Chronicle of Facts 2017-2025

"The Commissariat of the Heralds of the Gospel -- Chronicle of Facts 2017-2025" (Portuguese: "O Comissariado dos Arautos do Evangelho -- Cronica dos Fatos 2017-2025") is the book that serves as the primary source for this encyclopedia. Authored by Prof. Dr. Jose Manuel Jimenez Aleixandre and Sr. Dr. Juliane Vasconcelos Almeida Campos, the work documents with primary sources, annexes, and references the entire period from the Apostolic Visitation of 2017 to the end of the Commissariat in 2025. ISBN 978-65-01-80527-6.
