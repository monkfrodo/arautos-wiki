Friar Evaldo Xavier

Friar Evaldo Xavier was one of the collaborators of the Commissariat of the Heralds of the Gospel. His role is marked by a scandal involving compromising photographs, whose authenticity was confirmed by forensic verification. The case raises questions about the moral fitness of the collaborators chosen to supervise the Heralds.
